How do I get our District to be verified by Jola?

Dependent upon the amount of users from a school, yours will automatically be added to our verification quene through our ambassador program. We'll use the contact details that are found on your school websites to finalize things. You can check if your school is on the verification quene



How can I enable transcript based recommendations?

Jola Supports all major SIS providers. Some SIS providers require the student to install our web extension. If your SIS is not supported, students can just take a screenshot of their transcript. Jola will use its OCR capabilities to compensate.



Does Jola offer bulk discounts for Jola Go?

Absolutely. We offer major discounts to purchase Jola Go for all students in your school. Please email us at support@getjola.me.



Can I trust Jola with student data.

Absolutely. All accounts are private by default. If your school is verified, you can lock out portions of the settings panel (including the Public profile toggle).
