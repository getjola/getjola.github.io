How to pass that interview you've been dreading.

October 12th

Listen, we've all been there. You're application for your dream job has been accepted - and the last step is that darn interview. The last thing you want to do is completely blow it.


A few weeks ago you sent in your application to any employer you’ve dreamed of working for. Today you check your email says that they loved your application and that they would like to have an interview with you in a few weeks. You’re filled with happiness and immediately reply saying that you’ll be sure to be there.

But then seconds later you have this huge fear in yourself. You don’t want to mess up at the last step of getting into your job! But there really isn’t any need to panic - we have some advice to help you with completing this last step.

Some jobs may have a technical review - there are plenty of websites that have practice exercises that are quite similar to the one you will have in person. Try spending 1-2 hours a day just grinding away at these problems. Try doing a mock interview with friends, not only does this give you a sense of what the interview is like, but it will allow you to practice good communication techniques. Reflect on why you want to work for that company - most interviews will require you to say why you want to work for them. On the day of the interview make sure you are wearing formal clothing and that you are groomed properly. Make sure that you come early.

As Long as you follow our advice - you’ll do great on that interview.
