Privacy Policy

In order to provide our users the best experience we collect some information.

We store the following information from our users:

  • Account Type - This is either Employer, Student, or Admin.
  • First Name - Used to display on your profile and for people to find you.
  • Last Name - Used to display on your profile and for people to find you.
  • Zip Code - This is needed to show jobs near you & allows us to show you rich content that you care about.
  • Bio - This is some information that we can use to show on your profile to give people a general sense of who you are.
  • Grade - Certain careers might lock their listing for only certain grades - by requesting your grade we are able to enforce this.
  • Password Hash - No passwords are stored directly..everything is hashed.
  • Profile Picture - This is uploaded by you and is shown on your profile so people can easily recognize you.
