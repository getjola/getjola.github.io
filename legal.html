Terms of Service

In order to keep our platform clean and safe for our users, we expect you to follow these rules.

  • Users should not create more than 2 accounts.
  • Profanity should not be used anywhere on our platform.
  • You are not allowed to use an alternate account to bypass an account ban.
  • You are not allowed to share accounts. If you are an employer and want to share an account with other people, please contact us so we can make a group account for you.
  • Any images posted to our platform must be SFW.
  • Any job listings must be from the actual employer themselves.
  • Any information you enter in the Jola Platform must be true. Please don't lie about things like your name, grade, zip-code, and school. Not only are you breaking the rules, Jola isn't really going to benefit you as the results will not be fine tuned.
If we find that you have violated our Terms of Service we reserve the right to terminate your account. All memberships that are set to automatically renew will be cancelled.
